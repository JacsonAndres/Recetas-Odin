Introduction.

Pasta is a fundamental element in Italian gastronomy and has gained popularity around the world due to its versatility and delicious taste. In this collection of basic pasta recipes, we will discover three iconic dishes that have left their mark on the culinary world and in the hearts of many food lovers.

  • Lasagna
  • Macaroni and cheese
  • Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

In this collection of basic pasta recipes, we invite you to explore the world of Italian cuisine and enjoy the richness of flavors that these traditional dishes offer. From lasagna to smoked macaroni and cheese to spaghetti aglio e olio, these recipes will allow you to savor the very essence of Italian cuisine in the comfort of your own home. Enjoy!
